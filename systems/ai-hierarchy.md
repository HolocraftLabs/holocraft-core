# Holocraft AI Hierarchy

Holocraft uses a multi-agent AI collaboration model.

Different AI systems are assigned different operational responsibilities.

AI systems are treated as specialized tools inside a larger engineering workflow.

No AI system has unrestricted authority.

Human leadership remains the final authority layer.

---

# Authority Structure

Human Decision Maker
    ↓
Core Architecture Documents
    ↓
Architecture AI Systems
    ↓
Implementation AI Systems
    ↓
Automation Agents

Core documentation always overrides AI-generated suggestions.

---

---

# AI Operational Rules

AI systems must:

- preserve architectural consistency
- avoid unnecessary rewrites
- respect project standards
- follow existing system structure
- prioritize maintainability

AI systems must not:

- redesign stable systems unnecessarily
- introduce chaotic architecture changes
- replace core frameworks without approval
- bypass documented engineering standards

---

# Conflict Resolution

If multiple AI systems disagree:

1. Core documentation becomes the source of truth.
2. Architectural stability takes priority.
3. Simpler solutions are preferred.
4. Human decisions override all AI output.

---

# Studio Philosophy

AI is collaborative intelligence.

The purpose of AI inside Holocraft is to:

- accelerate execution
- improve engineering quality
- increase iteration speed
- reduce repetitive work
- assist decision making

AI exists to enhance builders, not replace them.
